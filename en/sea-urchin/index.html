Sea Urchin (Uni) Guide

Sea urchin, or uni (雲丹), is one of the most prized delicacies in East Asian cuisine. As Hong Kong and Macau's leading uni supplier, we provide comprehensive knowledge and premium products.

Sea Urchin Varieties

Bafun Uni (馬糞海膽)

Origin
Hokkaido, Japan
Season
Peak: June–August
Flavor
Rich, creamy, intensely sweet with deep umami
Best for
Premium sashimi, sushi nigiri, uni donburi

Murasaki Uni (紫海膽)

Origin
Various regions, Japan
Season
Year-round with seasonal peaks
Flavor
Delicate, light brininess, clean ocean finish
Best for
Sashimi, fine dining preparations

Canadian Red Sea Urchin

Origin
British Columbia, Canada
Season
Peak: October–May
Flavor
Large lobes, bold flavor, buttery texture
Best for
Sashimi, fusion cuisine, Western fine dining

Quality Grading

AA Grade

Perfect golden color, firm texture, no blemishes. The finest available — reserved for top omakase.

A Grade

Excellent quality with minor variations. Ideal for high-end sushi restaurants and premium dining.

B Grade

Good quality, suitable for cooking, pasta, and processed uni products.

Storage & Handling

  • Keep at 0–2°C at all times
  • Consume within 2–3 days of delivery
  • Never freeze fresh uni — texture degrades significantly
  • Store in original wooden tray, covered
  • Avoid direct contact with ice or water
