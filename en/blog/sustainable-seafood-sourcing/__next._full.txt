1. **Fishery is not overfished** — Population levels are healthy and sustainable
2. **Fishing practices minimize environmental impact** — Bycatch is managed, habitats protected
3. **Fishery is well-managed** — Rules are enforced, monitoring is transparent
4. **Supply chain integrity** — Chain of custody verified from boat to buyer

1b:T1033,To lock in sustainable sourcing at consistent pricing, use this contract framework:

**Key Contract Elements:**

**1. Certification Requirements**
```
Both parties agree to MSC/ASC certification for all seafood supplied.
Product must arrive with valid certification documentation.
If certification lapses, supplier must notify buyer within 48 hours.
No non-certified product may be substituted without prior written approval.
```

**2. Traceability Specifications**
```
Every shipment must include:
• Product name and grade (e.g., "MSC Canadian Red Sea Urchin, AA-Grade")
• Harvest date (for wild) or harvest/processing date (for farmed)
• Vessel name and license number (for wild-caught)
• Farm location (for farmed salmon)
• Cold chain temperature logs
• Certification number and issuing body
• Supplier contact for questions

Buyer retains all documentation for 12 months for compliance.
```

**3. Quality Assurance**
```
Product must meet stated grade and certification standards.
If product fails inspection (aroma test, visual assessment, taste test),
supplier must replace at no cost within 48 hours.
Repeat failures (>2 per quarter) permit buyer to cancel contract.
```

**4. Pricing Structure**
```
Base price: [per kg] for certified, grade-specified product
Seasonal adjustment: [±5%] based on market availability
Volume tier pricing: Available for commitments >20kg/week
Price locked: 6-month contract minimum; price reviewed quarterly

Example:
- Base price: HKD 800/kg for MSC Canadian Red Urchin (AA-grade)
- Volume tier: >20kg/week = HKD 740/kg
- Seasonal: June-September may see +5% (higher demand)
- Review: Q1/Q2/Q3/Q4 pricing adjustments announced 2 weeks prior
```

**5. Minimum Order & Frequency**
```
Minimum order: 10 kg per delivery
Delivery frequency: Weekly or bi-weekly (buyer specifies)
Notice period: Buyer provides 3 business days notice of quantity
Flexibility: Up to ±20% variation week-to-week without penalty
```

**6. Communication Protocol**
```
Weekly harvest reports: Supplier sends availability update every Friday
Pre-season planning: March (salmon) and May (sea urchin) consultations
Quality feedback: Buyer notifies supplier within 4 hours of quality issues
Market updates: Supplier alerts buyer to supply disruptions >3 days advance
Contact: Dedicated supplier contact; 24-hour response guarantee
```

**7. Sustainability Reporting**
```
Supplier agrees to provide:
• Annual sustainability report (sourcing practices, certifications)
• Carbon footprint calculation (optional but increasingly expected)
• Documentation for buyer's ESG reporting to corporate parent
• Quarterly certification status updates
```
